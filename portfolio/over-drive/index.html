Over Drive

Overview

Over Drive is a first person shooter that I was developing for PC. Over Drive is a project that is was worked on by myself as a side project I am doing in my spare time. With my love for first person shooters, I decided I wanted to take a stab at making my own fun FPS game. I have been working on Over Drive since the beginning of November 2015 and there is a lot I must tackle before this game is complete.

If continued, Over Drive will have multiple game modes the user can play such as Campaign, Survival, Boot Camp and possibly multiplayer. I would also be providing constant updates to the project and posting videos of my updates to show what's in stored for the game!
