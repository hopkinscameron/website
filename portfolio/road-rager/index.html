Road Rager

Overview

Road Rager is a 2D top down racing game built using Unity3D. In this game, the player controls their choice of car and uses their skills to race through traffic without causing accidents.

The players game score increases based on the time they can survive racing. To help the user, there are assest the user can pick up, such as a shield and a fireball, that allows the user to use these pickups to their advantage. The fireball gives the user on shot to shoot a fireball at an enemy car to stop the car from crashing into the player, causing them to lose. The shield pickup allows the user to surround themself with a shield that protects them for a short length of time. If an enemy car comes in contact with the shield, the car is destroyed, and so it the shield, but the player is safe, for now.

Play on different difficulty levels to test your skills and see what high score you can achieve!
